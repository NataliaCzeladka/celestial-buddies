Who we are?

We are a group of stargazers and space enthusiasts dedicated to exploring the wonders of the night sky.

Whether you're a seasoned astronomer or just starting your celestial journey, our club offers a welcoming community where you can learn, share, and marvel at the vastness of the universe. Through our regular meetups and stargazing events, we strive to foster a passion for astronomy and inspire a sense of awe and curiosity about the cosmos.

Join us as we embark on celestial adventures, observe distant galaxies, study celestial objects, and deepen our understanding of the mysteries beyond Earth.

Together, let's unlock the secrets of the universe and appreciate the beauty that lies millions of light-years away.

What we do?

We offer a range of exciting activities.

Our regular monthly meetups are held on the first Friday of every month at 6 pm. They provide a platform for members to share their knowledge, learn from experts, and explore fascinating topics related to astronomy and space exploration. Through our engaging classes, presentations and thought-provoking discussions, we aim to foster a vibrant learning environment where everyone can expand their understanding of the cosmos.

In addition to our regular meetups, we also organize stargazing events. Since the visibility of celestial objects is dependent on weather conditions, these events are often organized on relatively short notice. It allows us to make the most of clear skies and offer unique opportunities to observe planets and other astronomical phenomena through telescopes.

Where to find us?

For our engaging lectures, presentations and discussions, we gather at the Skegness Library, located at 23 Roman Bank. This vibrant venue provides us not only with a comfortable and welcoming space to delve into the fascinating world of astronomy, but also with a selection of our favourite astronomy magazines to keep us updated with the latest astronomical discoveries.

When it comes to our stargazing events, we invite you to meet us at the rooftop of the Gibraltar Point Cafe situated next to the car park at the Gibraltar Point. This unique vantage point offers unobstructed views of the night sky, making it the perfect spot for observing celestial wonders. Please note that our stargazing events are weather-dependent and may be organized on short notice, so keep an eye on our social media channels for updates.
