Upcoming Day Trips

National Space Centre, Leicester

The National Space Centre is a museum and educational resource covering the fields of space science and astronomy, along with a space research programme in partnership with the University of Leicester. It's also home to the Sir Patrick Moore Planetarium, the UK's largest fulldome planetarium.

The Royal Observatory, Greenwich

The Royal Observatory in Greenwich is the home to The Great Equatorial Telescope (the largest of its kind in the UK) and to the Peter Harrison Planetarium. One of the main atractions there is also to stand on the historic Prime Meridian Line and learn all about Greenwich Mean Time (GMT) and how Greenwich came to be the home of time.

Science Museum, London

The Science Museum’s astronomy and cosmology collection spans from the 10th century to the present day and contains a wide range of observation and demonstration equipment. Alongside the works held in the Library and Archives, they form one of the world’s best collections for studying the history of astronomy.

Regular Events

European AstroFest

European AstroFest is the UK’s premier astronomy conference and exhibition, featuring talks from leading astronomers and space scientists, plus three floors of exhibition space. It takes place at Kensington Conference and Events Centre, London.

Trips to the nearest observatories

We normally choose to observe major sky events like meteor showers, comets, eclipses and supermoons in the locations more suited to it than our humble rooftop. For this purpose, we regularly visit observatories in Cleethorpes and Lincoln.

Solstice at Stonehenge

Every year we are happy to witness the magic of the summer or winter solstice at Stonehenge, where ancient history and celestial wonder converge. Would you like to experience this extraordinary alignment of the sun's rays with the iconic stone monoliths with us?

Past Events

Practical Astronomy Show

The Practical Astronomy Show is a one-day show at the Kettering Conference and Exhibition Centre. The show aims to bring a selection of leading astronomy businesses, organisations and educational institutions together under one roof. Astronomy products and services will be available for purchase from various stands.

Meet a NASA engineer

This online event allows you to gain insights into being a NASA engineer from four professionals from NASA Goddard Space Flight Center and NASA Langley Research Center. They share stories about how they became engineers and the interesting things they do at NASA.

Building a space telescope model

You are going to follow European Space Agency's instructions to build a 3D CAD model of the Webb Space Telescope using Fusion 360. Creating it involves modelling techniques, rendering and a sculpt extension activity for more experienced users of Fusion 360.
